Chi master level
the foundation

Let’s be honest: as a healer, it is easy to spend all of our time running around helping and healing others, and little time nurturing our own wellbeing. But if you want to go deeper into any healing system, if you want to become the best healer and teacher you can become, then the foundation of everything you do is your personal practice. This is what sustains you when you are going through challenges.

This is what helps you to stay energetically grounded when working on energetically unbalanced clients and students. This is what enables you to channel ever-greater quantities of chi and truly amp up the power of the healing work that you do. But if your foundation is weak, if you don’t tend to yourself first, then when times get rough, your inner balance and health may suffer.

In the Master Level, we therefore begin by teaching you several advanced techniques for greater emotional wellbeing, clarity and inner balance. Techniques include:

the 12 pillars of spiritual health

Discover the 12 key ingredients that lead to a healer’s wellbeing and find out how to optimize and balance all of them.

the chi activation precepts:

Your mind is going to fill up with something. The question is simply what? If you don’t tend to the information flowing into it, then you get the societal default. You get all of the junk in the news. You get the whining of your friends.
You get the hopes, dreams and theories of dissatisfied people. The sum total of all of this becomes your mind’s ‘operating system’.

It becomes the filter through which it sees and interprets the world. To be happy, however, to be the best version of YOU that you can be, you need to take charge of your mental ‘operating system’ by feeding your mind the ‘right’ kind of stuff. It is here that the Chi Activation Precepts come in.
Go through them in the morning before you start your day, and you will be programming yourself to make the most of it, programming yourself to tune in to the brightest of YOU that you can be. The precepts will be your anchor when your day gets stormy.

They will be your rocket ship when you want to fly. Along with ‘vibrational meditation’, they will help your day flow more brightly.

‘Core Being’ Meditation:

They say you don’t want to get to the top of the ladder only to realize that it is leaning up against the wrong wall. So often in life, we run helter-skelter in a direction, we work super hard for a result, only to realize later on that while ‘successful’, our success doesn’t really fulfil us. In fact, in many cases, we realize that we weren’t really doing things because they were ‘true to who we are’, but rather, we did them out of social obligation, expectation, or because we had absorbed societal values – values that weren’t true to us!

In the ‘core being’ meditation, you learn to connect to the core of your being. Once you do this, then making the right kind of life decisions becomes far easier. No longer are you choosing a direction in reaction to the world around you. Rather, you are being guided by your inner being, by your higher wisdom.

Intuitive Healing:

Removing Healing Blocks:

Learning to remove ‘healing blocks’ is therefore critical to the success of a healer and something we work on in the course.

the art of teaching

You can give a person a fish, or you can teach them to fish. So goes the old saying, and it is as true as ever with healing. You can give someone a healing session, and it will certainly help clear energy blocks, grant insights, and improve a general sense of wellbeing (to name just a tiny fraction of the benefits), but if you want long-term, sustained healing, nothing beats teaching someone to heal themselves. For teach them to connect to energy frequencies, and they can give themselves as much healing as they desire, each and every day.

Over time, all of this healing accumulates and compounds, thus creating the kind of shifts that are hard to achieve with only a few healing sessions – no matter how good the practitioner.What’s more, when a person can heal themselves, they feel empowered. This shifts their perception of the world and helps them understand that they never need be a ‘victim’. They always have the power to shift their energetic and emotional state and to help others shift theirs.
For the founder of Chi Activation, Jeremy O’Carroll, this is one of the greatest gifts we could ever give anyone - Indeed, it is a life-changing gift.

In your Master Level course, you will learn how to do Chi Activation attunements, and how to teach this flexible and powerful method to others. Teaching others is one of the most satisfying things that you can do. Empowering them to heal themselves makes your healing work truly meaningful. It will also – thanks to the ripple effect – help your students’ family, friends and all those who come in contact with them. If you want to make a difference in the world, if you want to help raise the planet’s vibration, then teaching others to heal is one of the most powerful ways to do so.

Apart from the mechanics of teaching, this section will also teach you about the ‘psychology of teaching’ and how to avoid the most common mistakes even experienced healers make!
