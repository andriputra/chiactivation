channel your healing frequencies

Chi Activation Level 2 is where your ability to channel healing energy really ramps up. By leveraging your connection to the five key energy frequencies you tapped into in level 1, you now learn to tune into almost any frequency you desire.
In other words, in Chi Activation Level 2, there is no limit to the amount of frequencies you can channel. Better still, once you learn to tap into healing frequencies at will, you are then ready to learn the art of ‘vibrational balancing’.
To understand vibrational balancing, think of noise cancellation technology. To reduce background sounds, it uses what you may see as an inverse sound wave (technically, a sound that is ‘phase-inverted’ by 180 degrees). So you have a sound wave. You fire off its opposite, and the two of them neutralize each other, leaving no sound.
The principle of vibrational healing is the same, only instead of balancing sound waves, we are balancing energy frequencies.
This is made clearer when we think of organs. They all have a healthy vibrational range, and if they begin to vibrate outside of this range, health issues occur. So with vibrational healing, the idea is to help reattune the body, mind, emotions, etc., to a healthy frequency by balancing the unhealthy frequency with a ‘counter frequency’.
Like a tuning fork, this counter frequency then draws the imbalance into healthy alignment. Of course, to be capable of finding the right counter frequency, you need to be able to access a huge range of energies, and that is exactly what Chi Activation Level 2 teaches you to do.
