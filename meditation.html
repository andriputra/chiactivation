Meditation in Conjunction with
Chi Activation Healing

for powerful results

tune in with your inner self

One of the great things about Chi Activation Healing is you can use it to enhance your meditation practice.

To meditate while channeling Chi Activation energy, students learn to tune deeply into the frequency they are connecting to. Since opening up to energy is a deeply pleasurable experience, you can use this pleasure to anchor yourself in deep meditation.

As humans, we gravitate towards pleasure and shy away from pain. So the greater the pleasure you experience while healing, the more you will naturally want to tune into and focus on this pleasure – something that will not only heighten your experience (what you focus on grows), but also help your mind to stay in the present moment. Your mind gets distracted because it is either trying to improve your life, or because it is pondering how to avoid something that will make it worse.

learn to live in the moment

You meditate, and your mind starts thinking about what you can eat for dinner (something that will potentially improve your life), or it thinks about paying your car registration (if you don’t do that and you lose your licence or get fined, your life will get worse).

In either case, it distracts you from the present moment. But when you luxuriate in the pleasure of the moment, in the exquisite energy that is coursing through your body while practising Chi Activation, then you will want to stay put where you are. You want to stay immersed in the pleasurable sensations.

As a result, it will be harder for your mind to pull you away from what you are doing since you won’t want to stop doing it! And if your mind does distract you for a moment, you’ll want to get straight back to where you were.

meditate with chi activation energy

So when you practice Chi Activation Meditation, you’ll typically find it easier than normal to stay in a meditative state because the strong flow of pleasurable energy makes it easier to anchor yourself in the present moment.

Better still, when you meditate with Chi Activation energy, your body will fill up with invigorating, healing energy that will uplift your entire day. Do Chi Activation ‘healing meditation’ in the morning, and you’ll feel a difference in your body and mood.

You’ll be more energized, and you’ll be more balanced. You’ll be happier, and you’ll be mentally clearer and brighter.
